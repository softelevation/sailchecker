Yacht description

3-cabin sailing yacht Beneteau 41 - Targa was built in 2017 and it is docked in Marina d’Empuriabrava, Spain. Targa can accommodate up to 8 people in 3 double cabins and a saloon with 2 extra beds. Pillows and blankets are provided during the yacht rental.

Sailing Yacht Targa offers 2 toilets and a shower.

Beneteau features Bimini and Bow thruster. It also boasts Audio system, Outside speakers and Inside speakers. Fully-equipped kitchen includes Oven, Cooker, Sink and Kitchen utensils.

This yacht is operated in Spain by charter Dream Yacht Charter - Spain.

Information about marina

Marina d’Empuriabrava is situated nearby Empuriabrava, Spain.

It is located 67 kilometres from the nearest airport.

Marina d’Empuriabrava boasts a wide range of facilities for sailors, including an ATM, WiFi, a launderette, an exchange office, a car park, a petrol station, a toilet and showers.

It also offers a restaurant and a supermarket. All berths are connected to water and electricity.
